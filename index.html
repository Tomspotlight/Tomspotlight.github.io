Spotlight Health is a Bio-tech company dedicated to developing diagnostics tools by combining biology, engineering and data science

1 in 10 Women and those assigned female at birth suffer from endometriosis while of reproductive age.Priorities for endometriosis research: recommendations from an international consensus workshop. Rogers et al.

On average it takes 8 Years from onset of symptoms to diagnosis.World Health Organization, 2018; Zondervan et al., 2020

During this time endometriosis sufferers often endure:

  • Severe pelvic pain
  • Difficulty conceiving
  • Nausea and gastric symptoms, especially during period
  • Decline in mental wellbeing resulting in anxiety and depression

This often results in a huge impact on a sufferer's quality of life.

What if we could leverage the power of data and machine learning to both speed up diagnosis but also increase accessibility to diagnosis.

This has the potential to both reduce the burden to healthcare systems and improve patient outcomes.
