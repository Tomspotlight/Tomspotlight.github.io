Spotlight Health is a Newcastle-upon-Tyne based health tech company founded in 2023, dedicated to developing groundbreaking AI-powered technology for diagnosing endometriosis using data and scan images. Our mission is to reduce time to diagnosis and increase accessibility to high quality diagnostics, ultimately leading to improved health outcomes for women.

1 in 10 Women of those assigned female at birth suffer from endometriosis while of reproductive age.Priorities for endometriosis research: recommendations from an international consensus workshop. Rogers et al.

On average it takes 8 Years from onset of symptoms to diagnosis.World Health Organization, 2018; Zondervan et al., 2020

During this time endometriosis sufferers often endure:

  • Severe pelvic pain
  • Difficulty conceiving
  • Nausea and gastric symptoms, especially during periods
  • Decline in mental wellbeing resulting in anxiety and depression

This often results in a huge impact on a sufferer's quality of life.

The current gold standard diagnostic is an invasive surgical procedure.

What if we could leverage the power of data and machine learning to both speed up diagnosis, perform the diagnosis non-invasively, and increase accessibility.

This has the potential to both reduce the burden to healthcare systems and improve patient outcomes.
