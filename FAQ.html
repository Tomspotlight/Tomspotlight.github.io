Frequently Asked Questions


What are you making?

We are building a tool to quickly diagnose endometriosis without the need for invasive surgery.


How does it work?

We use a broad set of information, provided by the patient, and use it alongside the analysis of ultrasound scans to look for signs typical of other positive endometriosis cases.


When will it be ready?

We're not sure yet, we're taking our time and making sure we have a solid platform before we allow it out into the wild.


How can I help?

We love talking to endometriosis experts, if you're a gynaecologist, or a doctor with a special interest in endometriosis, particularly in the UK, please get in touch.


Are you looking for investment?

We are working on our funding plan to make sure we've the right support at the right time.
